01 — About Me

The Story
So Far

I am Harith Sivaram, a first-year Computer Science Engineering student at the Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore — and I have been fascinated by programming since my very first encounter with Python in Grade 8. That early introduction was not just a subject; it was a spark that set off a genuine passion for building, problem-solving, and thinking critically.

Through school, I channelled that curiosity into self-directed projects, teaching myself to turn ideas into functional software. The process of taking a blank file and turning it into something useful — a system that works, that solves a real problem — is something I find deeply rewarding. It is that instinct to build that continues to drive me forward.

Today, my focus has sharpened considerably. I am drawn most powerfully to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning — fields where programming meets scientific rigour, where well-crafted code can yield insights, automate decisions, and genuinely change how the world operates. I believe my strongest work is still ahead of me, and I am here to build it.

Train Booking System
